How Our Integrated Program Works

Complete Reconstruction Through Partnership

Dreams Rebuild Volunteers Association is part of a unique three-organization partnership that provides comprehensive reconstruction support from design to completion:

Step 1: Professional Design (DRVA)

Our volunteer architects and engineers create custom, professional architectural designs tailored to each family's specific needs. All design services are provided completely free by licensed professionals who donate their expertise to Ukraine's reconstruction.

Step 2: Financial Support (Dreams Realized Charity)

Families in our program receive $5,000 USD or equivalent value in home essentials including appliances, textiles, and kitchen equipment. Military families and families of fallen heroes receive additional premium furniture packages.

Step 3: Quality Construction (Sunwell Group)

Sunwell Group provides high-quality building materials and professional construction crews to bring the designs to life. Projects are completed within 90-120 days using quality materials and experienced construction teams.

Step 4: Ongoing Support

Our volunteer architects provide construction oversight, design clarifications, and quality inspections to ensure designs are properly executed. Families can also participate in tourism homestay programs to generate additional income.

Why an Integrated Approach?
By working with all three organizations together, we ensure quality control throughout the entire reconstruction process. Professional design, adequate financial support, and quality construction combine to give families homes they can be proud of‚Äîbuilt to EU standards and designed for the future.

About Us

Our Mission

Dreams Rebuild Volunteers Association (DRVA) was founded on a simple belief: every Ukrainian family deserves access to world-class architectural design services, regardless of their ability to pay. We bring together licensed architects, structural engineers, and construction professionals who volunteer their expertise to create beautiful, safe, and sustainable homes for Ukraine's future.

Who We Are

We are a network of professional volunteers‚Äîarchitects with decades of experience, young engineers eager to contribute their skills, and construction experts who understand how to design for efficiency and quality. Our volunteers come from Canada, the United States, Europe, and Ukraine itself, united by a commitment to Ukraine's reconstruction.

Our Approach

We've created an integrated partnership model that ensures families receive complete reconstruction support:

Dreams Rebuild Volunteers Association provides professional architectural and engineering design services completely free of charge through our volunteer network.

Dreams Realized Charity provides direct financial assistance and home essentials to families in our reconstruction program.

Sunwell Group supplies quality building materials and manages construction with experienced crews, ensuring designs are properly executed.

Our Standards

Professional Excellence: All designs are created by licensed architects and engineers with proven expertise.

Cultural Sensitivity: We honor Ukrainian architectural heritage while embracing modern sustainability and efficiency.

European Standards: Our designs comply with EU building codes, preparing Ukraine for accession.

Family-Centered: We work directly with families to understand their needs and create personalized solutions.

Sustainable Future: We design for energy efficiency, durability, and adaptability to future needs.
